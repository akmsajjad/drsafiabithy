    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to the most common questions about our services, treatments, and what you can expect during your journey with us.

    What services do you offer?

    We offer a wide range of aesthetic and cosmetic treatments, including Botox, fillers, thread lifts, laser therapies, hair restoration, body contouring, and skin revitalization.

    Are the treatments safe?

    Yes, all of our treatments are performed using the latest technology and are administered by highly trained professionals to ensure your safety and comfort.

    How do I know which treatment is right for me?

    During your initial consultation, Dr. Safia Bithy will assess your needs, discuss your goals, and recommend the most suitable treatment plan tailored to your unique requirements.

    Is there any downtime after treatment?

    Most of our treatments are minimally invasive, meaning you can typically resume your daily activities immediately after your session. However, some procedures may require a short recovery period.

    How long will the results last?

    The longevity of results depends on the specific treatment, but our team will provide you with detailed aftercare instructions to help you maintain your results for as long as possible.

    Do you offer consultations?

    Yes, we offer consultations to discuss your aesthetic goals and guide you in choosing the best treatment options. Book your consultation online or call us to schedule.
